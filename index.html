TechBookRadio

Creator-owned tool that publishes our own short tech & AI videos to our own social accounts.

What this is

TechBook Radio is a personal creator channel that produces short vertical videos (about 60 seconds) summarizing technology books and explaining AI/tech news in Vietnamese. This website also documents the channel's in-house publishing tool: a small utility the channel owner uses to upload the channel's own finished videos to the owner's own connected accounts on TikTok, YouTube Shorts and Facebook Reels, using each platform's official API.

How the publishing tool works

  1. Connect account. The channel owner signs in to their own TikTok account through TikTok Login Kit and authorizes the tool (scope user.info.basic). The tool shows the connected account's username and avatar so the owner confirms it is the right account.
  2. Prepare the video. An already-produced TechBook Radio video, with its title, description and hashtags, is selected. The owner can review and edit these before sending.
  3. Upload. Using the TikTok Content Posting API (video.upload / video.publish), the tool uploads the video to the owner's own TikTok account, where the owner picks the privacy level and confirms the post.
The tool acts only on accounts the channel owner has explicitly authorized. It never accesses, collects, or posts on behalf of any other user, and it adds no cross-platform watermark.

Data & privacy

The tool uses the TikTok data strictly needed to publish (account identifiers from user.info.basic and OAuth tokens), stored locally on the owner's computer and never sold or shared. Full details — including what we collect, how long we keep it, and how to request deletion — are in our Privacy Policy.
