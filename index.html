About

TechBook Radio is a personal creator channel that produces short vertical videos summarizing technology books and AI/tech news in Vietnamese. The videos are published to our own channels on TikTok, YouTube Shorts and Facebook Reels.

Our publishing tool

We use an in-house tool to upload our own finished videos to our own connected social accounts. The tool acts only on accounts the channel owner has explicitly authorized, using official platform APIs. It does not access, collect, or post on behalf of any other user.

Contact

For any question about this service, contact the channel owner via our Facebook page.
