Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the TechBook Radio content-publishing tool ("the tool", "we", "us") handles data. The tool is operated by the owner of the TechBook Radio channel to publish the channel's own videos to the channel owner's own social media accounts on TikTok, YouTube and Facebook. It is intended for the channel owner's own use only.

1. Data we collect from TikTok

When the channel owner connects their TikTok account through TikTok Login Kit and the Content Posting API, we receive and use only:

  • Basic account info via the user.info.basic scope — the account open_id, display name and avatar — shown in the tool so the owner can confirm they are publishing to the correct account.
  • OAuth access & refresh tokens — granted during login, used solely to call TikTok's official publishing API on the owner's own account.

We do not request or collect followers, messages, analytics, or any data about other TikTok users.

2. How we use the data

The data above is used for one purpose only: to authenticate the channel owner and upload the owner's own videos (with their titles, descriptions and hashtags) to the owner's own connected accounts. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

3. Storage & security

Access and refresh tokens are stored locally on the channel owner's own computer, in a local configuration file, and are used only to call the platforms' official APIs. Data is never sold, rented, shared with, or transmitted to any third party.

4. Data retention & deletion

Tokens are kept only as long as the tool is in use. The channel owner can:

  • Revoke access at any time from TikTok's Settings → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions, after which the tool can no longer act on the account; and
  • Delete all stored data immediately by removing the local configuration file.

To request deletion of any data associated with this application, email quangnd310794@gmail.com and we will delete it within 30 days.

5. Data we do not collect

The tool does not collect, store, or process personal data of viewers or of any other users. It does not read other users' content, build profiles, or run analytics on third parties.

6. Third-party platforms

Data handled by TikTok, YouTube, Facebook and other connected platforms is also governed by each platform's own privacy policy, including the TikTok Privacy Policy.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time; the "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

8. Contact

Privacy questions or data-deletion requests: quangnd310794@gmail.com (or via our Facebook page).
