Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the TechBook Radio content-publishing tool handles data. The tool is operated by the owner of the TechBook Radio channel for publishing the channel's own videos to the channel owner's own social media accounts.

1. Data we access

To publish videos, the tool uses access tokens that the account owner grants through the official login flows of platforms such as TikTok, YouTube and Facebook. These tokens allow the tool to upload videos and captions to the owner's own connected accounts.

2. How tokens are stored

Access and refresh tokens are stored locally on the channel owner's own computer (in a local configuration file) and are used only to call the platforms' official publishing APIs. Tokens are never sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party.

3. Data we do not collect

The tool does not collect, store, or process personal data of viewers or of any other users. It does not read other users' content, build profiles, or run analytics on third parties. It acts solely on the channel owner's own accounts.

4. Content we publish

The only content published is original videos produced by TechBook Radio, together with their titles, descriptions and hashtags.

5. Data retention & deletion

Locally stored tokens can be revoked at any time from the relevant platform's settings (e.g. the platform's connected-apps page), after which the tool can no longer act on the account. Deleting the local configuration file removes all stored tokens.

6. Third-party policies

Data handled by TikTok, YouTube, Facebook and other connected platforms is governed by each platform's own privacy policy.

7. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent via our Facebook page.
